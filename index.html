Who We Are

A legacy of storytelling

Vidisha Productions Pvt. Ltd. was established in 2002 as a production house dedicated to crafting feature films, television serials, and digital content that resonates across India and beyond.

Wingspan Motion Pictures LLP is our sister concern — and the banner under which all future content is produced — carrying forward two decades of experience with a renewed creative vision for Indian cinema. The Wingspan name builds on the legacy of Vidisha Productions, bringing the same creative commitment to a new chapter.

We are a registered member of the Indian Motion Pictures Producers' Association, and our team of experienced professionals brings deep expertise in every stage of film production.

The Team

Meet the Producers

SJ
Sunit Jawadekar
Producer · Co-Founder

Sunit Jawadekar is an entrepreneur with 35+ years of experience across the print and media industries. He scaled his family's printing business before pivoting to entertainment, founding Vidisha Productions Pvt. Ltd. in 2002 — producing television serials and feature films. In 2014, he launched a sister concern, Wingspan Motion Pictures LLP, under which he plans to drive all future productions.

wingspanmopictures@gmail.com
NK
Nivedita Kothare
Producer · Co-Founder

Nivedita Kothare is a creative professional with a background spanning corporate operations, graphic design, children's publishing, and travel. She founded her own design venture, working with labels including HMV and Sony Music, and managed international tours with Thomas Cook. In 2004, she joined Sunit Jawadekar in media and entertainment, bringing creative and operational depth to Wingspan Motion Pictures.
