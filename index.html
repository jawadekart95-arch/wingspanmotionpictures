Who We Are

A legacy of storytelling

Vidisha Productions Pvt. Ltd. was established in 2002 as a production house dedicated to crafting feature films, television serials, and digital content that resonates across India and beyond.

Wingspan Motion Pictures LLP is our sister concern under whose banner all future content is produced — carrying forward two decades of experience with a renewed creative vision for Indian cinema.

We are a registered member of the Indian Motion Pictures Producers' Association, and our team of experienced professionals brings deep expertise in every stage of film production.
